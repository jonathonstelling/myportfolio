Bridge Competition

Design Team: Jonathon Stelling, Alexander Vescio, Joseph Norton, Aidan Harris

Winners of the 2013 bridge competition

Concept

We evaluated all our designs using solidworks stress test simulation, we found that this bridge was our strongest concept and was estimated to fail at a load of 8kg.

Manufacturing & Testing

Whilst designing and manufacturing our bridge we decided that precision and accuracy were paramount to a successful build. To achieve this we created technical drawings using solidworks. We used these during the marking out process and achieved the correct angles using a machine disk sander for high precision.

Bridge weight: 24g Estimated load to taken: 8kg

When testing our bridge took a total load of 12kg, Our bridge held exactly 500 times its own weight.
