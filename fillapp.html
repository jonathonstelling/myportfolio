Fillapp

A smartphone app designed to utilize unused capacity in businesses which mostly incur fixed costs. Simplified interface enables firms to login and post last minute deals for spaces that have not been booked yet, in a variety of sectors such as; restaurants, cinemas, theatres, sporting events.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to create a virtual integrity between businesses with high fixed costs that are no capable of filling unsold services and those customers that enjoy last minute discounted deals. To provide an app that is easily accessible by all types of digital device users with its simplified design. Creation of a secure platform, where last minute deals can be offered and sealed, leaving both; businesses and consumers satisfied.‚Äù

Objectives

Year 1: Launch the App on Android and IOS platforms targeted towards New Jersey, Philadelphia and New York States for restaurants only.

Year 2: Enter different markets; cinemas, theatres, concerts and sports events. Spread the App across the East coast of the United States. Start charging consumer.

Year 3: Establish FillApp in all possible sectors in which fixed costs are dominants. Expand on to European countries.

Service and Customer Benefits

FillApp offers discounts to consumers. FillApp advertises offers from other companies. FillApp sells a platform connecting businesses with spontaneously open capacities to customers. Our product is benefiting participating merchants helping them to cover their fixed costs. The app is enabling the merchants to implement a form of second degree price discrimination (versioning) in order to extract a larger share of consumer surplus in their market. Instead of charging a lower uniform price in order to fill their capacities, merchants can now price discriminate by charging different prices to different customer groups. The prices can thus be closer to the individual marginal willingness to pay and the entrepreneur is able to transform more customer utility into profit. The customer groups reveal their marginal willingness to pay by choosing whether they pay full price for secure seats (these are willing to pay more) or whether they wait to pay less and take the risk of not getting a seat. As it becomes clear, the benefit from our app to the merchants is that their discounts are targeted correctly at customers who without the discount would not have purchased. With the implemented versioning price discrimination, customers reveal their type simply by what they purchase and the business owner can offer high and low prices to the correct customer group.
