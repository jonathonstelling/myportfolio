Radial Engine

Date Completed: 2000

A CAD model of a Radial Engine I made for a CAD assignment. I had to create a model which had at least two moving parts, weighed under 100g and would be able to be 3D printed as an assembly. My design was selected to be shown at the Northumbria University Faculty Show 2015, where my CAD model, renderings and animations were put on show

After researching into the tolerances of a 3D printer and what gaps needed to be left so that support material could be removed and the assembly would be able to move freely, I was able to create a 3 cylinder radial engine complete with moving valves

Description of the product:
